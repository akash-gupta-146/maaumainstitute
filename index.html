FROM THE DESK OF DIRECTOR

Maa Uma Electronic Training Institute is one among the foremost institutes of Madhya Pradesh in Skill Development Training of Technical, Electrical, Electronics, Beauty Culture and Garments Making.The institute was established for providing employment / self-employment to unemployed citizens by modern mythology and giving technical & practical education, whose operation is done by a committee of experienced and distinguished personalities.Institute is registered under M.P. Government act 1973 no. 44. Institute is ISO 9001-2008 certified. And the courses are govern by Skill Development and Modular Employable Skill Schemes in the institute.
